게시물 작성(jarfile에 담긴 쿠키 정보 읽어와서 사용)

curl "localhost:8000/board/show?id=1&page=1&limit=10&asc=true"

코멘트 작성
curl -d "content=TESTcontent" --cookie jarfile localhost:8000/board/comment/1/create
curl -d "content=TESTcontent" --cookie jarfile localhost:8000/board/comment/create?id=1
curl -d "content=TESTcontent&board_id=1" --cookie jarfile localhost:8000/board/comment/create
코멘트 수정
curl -d "content=ModifiedContent&id=1" --cookie jarfile localhost:8000/board/comment/1/update
curl -d "content=ModifiedContent&id=1" --cookie jarfile localhost:8000/board/comment/update?id=1
curl -d "content=ModifiedContent&id=1&board_id=1" --cookie jarfile localhost:8000/board/comment/update
코멘트 삭제

curl -d "id=1" --cookie jarfile localhost:8000/board/comment/delete?id=1
